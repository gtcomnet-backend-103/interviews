# Phase 5: Cultural Fit & QA (10 mins)
**Goal:** Evaluate how they work within a team and handle pressure.

## 1. Handling Disagreements
- *Question:* "Tell me about a time you strongly disagreed with a UI/UX designer's mockup because it would be too difficult or unperformant to build. How did you resolve it?"
  - *Good signal:* Shows empathy. They don't just say "no". They explain the technical constraints to the designer and offer a compromise that achieves the business goal without sacrificing app performance.

> 💡 **Interviewer Cheat Sheet:** Designers sometimes create beautiful screens that will run incredibly slow on older phones. A good Senior Frontend Engineer will push back politely and offer faster alternatives.

## 2. QA & Wrap Up
- "We have about 5 minutes left. What questions do you have for me about the team, the company, or the tech stack?"
  - *Good signal:* Asks about the CI/CD pipeline for the frontend, how the team handles tech debt, or what the biggest current UI challenges are.
