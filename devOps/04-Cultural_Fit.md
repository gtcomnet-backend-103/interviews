# Phase 4: Cultural Fit & Behavioral (10 mins)
**Goal:** Evaluate how they work within a team and handle pressure.

- *Question:* "Tell me about a time when you broke production. What happened, how did you fix it, and what did you learn?"
  - *Good signal:* Takes ownership, explains the post-mortem process, and implemented automated guardrails to prevent it from happening again.
- *Question:* "How do you handle a situation where a developer insists on deploying code that you know is failing security checks or tests?"
  - *Good signal:* Empathy, communication, balancing business needs with security, and pairing with the developer to fix the issue.
