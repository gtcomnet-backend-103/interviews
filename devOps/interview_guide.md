# DevOps Engineer Interview Guide (Mid-to-Senior)

## Interview Overview
- **Duration**: 75-90 minutes
- **Format**: 1-on-1 Structured Interview
- **Core Stack Focus**: AWS, Terraform, Kubernetes (EKS), Docker, CI/CD (GitHub Actions / GitLab CI)

---

## Interview Phases
The interview is split across the following sections:
1. `01-Introduction.md`
2. `02-Knowledge_Session.md`
3. `03-Technical_Interview.md`
4. `04-Cultural_Fit.md`
5. `05-QA_and_Wrap_Up.md`

---

## Scoring Rubric

- **Junior Level (Red Flags):** Struggles with core K8s/Terraform concepts. Proposes manual steps or monolithic Terraform. Lacks a blameless mindset.
- **Mid-Level (Passing):** Solid knowledge base. Solves the core architecture scenario but might need hints for zero-downtime migrations. Demonstrates accountability.
- **Senior Level (Strong Hire):** Proactively addresses security, IRSA, and GitOps. Designs a seamless cutover. Exhibits strong leadership and mentorship qualities.
