# Phase 1: Introduction (5-10 mins)
**Goal:** Make the candidate feel comfortable and set the stage for the interview.

- **Interviewer Intro:** Briefly introduce yourself, your role, and what the team does.
- **Candidate Intro:** Ask them to give a 2-3 minute summary of their background and their most recent DevOps/SRE role.
- **Agenda:** Outline the format of this interview (Introduction -> Knowledge Session -> System Design Scenario -> Q&A).
