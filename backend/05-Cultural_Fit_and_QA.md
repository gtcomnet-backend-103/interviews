# Phase 5: Cultural Fit & QA (10 mins)
**Goal:** Evaluate how they work within a team and handle pressure.

## 1. Handling Disasters
- *Question:* "Tell me about a time when a migration failed in production or a critical bug brought down the API. What happened and how did you resolve it?"
  - *Good signal:* Takes ownership. Explains the rollback process, the post-mortem, and what automated tests they added to prevent it from happening again.

> 💡 **Interviewer Cheat Sheet:** Look for a "blameless" mindset. They shouldn't blame the junior dev who wrote the bug; they should blame the CI/CD pipeline that allowed the bug to get to production.

## 2. QA & Wrap Up
- "We have about 5 minutes left. What questions do you have for me about the team, the company, or the tech stack?"
  - *Good signal:* Asks about the deployment process (Envoyer/Forge/Docker), test coverage (Pest/PHPUnit), or how the team handles technical debt.
